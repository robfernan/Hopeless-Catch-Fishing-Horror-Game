# Quick Start - Hopeless Catch

Get started fishing in 2 minutes!

## Play the Game

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Click "Start Game"
3. You're ready to fish!

## Basic Controls

| Key | What It Does |
|-----|--------------|
| WASD or Arrows | Walk around |
| SPACE | Cast/Reel/Hook (main action) |
| B | Open tackle box to change bait |
| TAB | Open journal to see catches |
| H | Rest in cabin to advance time |
| ESC | Pause/Menu |

## How to Fish

1. **Get Ready**: Press SPACE once
   - Message: "READY: Press SPACE again to cast"

2. **Start Casting**: Press SPACE again
   - Message: "Casting... Hold to build power"
   - Power meter appears

3. **Cast**: Press SPACE again
   - Line and bobber appear in water
   - Message: "Waiting for a bite..."

4. **Wait for Bite**: Bobber will flash red
   - Message: "BITE! Press SPACE to hook!"

5. **Hook the Fish**: Press SPACE
   - Message: "Reeling... X%"

6. **Reel In**: Press SPACE repeatedly
   - Reel progress increases
   - When complete: "You caught the fish!"

7. **See Your Catch**: Catch window appears
   - Shows fish name, description, and lore

## Tips

- **Worms are default bait** - Good for all fish
- **Minnows attract faster bites** - Use for quick fishing
- **Cheese is slower** - Use for patience
- **Fish change at night** - Day fish are peaceful, night fish are mysterious
- **Check your journal** - See all fish you've caught and their lore
- **Rest in cabin** - Advance time to see day/night cycle

## Settings

Press ESC to open menu, then click "Settings":
- **Peaceful Mode**: Turn off night creatures if you want pure relaxation
- **Volume**: Adjust music and sound levels

## Troubleshooting

**No music playing?**
- Click on the game first (browser autoplay policy)
- Check volume in settings

**Can't see the bobber?**
- Make sure you cast (press SPACE 3 times)
- Check it's in the water, not floating above

**Fish not biting?**
- Wait a bit longer
- Try different bait
- Check it's not nighttime (night fish are different)

**Game running slow?**
- Close other browser tabs
- Try a different browser
- Check FPS counter (should be 60)
