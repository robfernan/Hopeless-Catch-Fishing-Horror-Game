# Hopeless Catch - Game Design Document

## Vision

**Hopeless Catch** is a cozy horror fishing game that blends relaxation with mystery. By day, it's a peaceful fishing adventure. By night, the waters reveal their secrets.

## Core Concept

### Cozy Elements
- Peaceful pixel art aesthetic
- Relaxing fishing mechanics
- Progression and upgrades
- Companion bonding system
- Safe spaces (cabin) to retreat to

### Horror Elements
- Atmospheric shifts at night
- Strange fish species
- Mysterious lore and discoveries
- Environmental storytelling
- Rare legendary catches with unsettling descriptions

## Game Loop

### Day Phase (Peaceful)
1. Wake up in cabin
2. Go fishing at current location
3. Catch common fish species
4. Upgrade gear with earnings
5. Bond with companion
6. Return to cabin to rest

### Night Phase (Mysterious)
1. Venture out after dark
2. Encounter rare/strange creatures
3. Discover lore entries
4. Experience anomalies
5. Retreat to safety if needed
6. Rest and reflect

## Progression System

### Companion Bonding
- **Level 1-5**: Companion grows closer
- **Unlocks**: Special dialogue, warnings, celebrations
- **Bonding Points**: +10 per normal catch, +50 per legendary
- **Benefits**: Better fishing luck, emotional support

### Gear Upgrades
- **Rod Quality**: Affects casting accuracy and distance
- **Reel Strength**: Affects tension management
- **Tackle Quality**: Attracts rarer fish
- **Progression**: Unlock better gear as you catch more fish

### Location Unlocks
- **Lake** (Start): 5 day fish, 4 night creatures
- **Forest Pond** (5 unique fish): New species, deeper lore
- **Mountain Stream** (3 lore entries): Rare fish, challenging catches
- **Mysterious Depths** (1 legendary): Endgame content, ultimate secrets

### Lore Discovery
- **Journal Entries**: Piece together the lake's history
- **Fish Descriptions**: Learn about each creature
- **Environmental Clues**: Discover secrets through exploration
- **Unlocks**: New locations, special events, endings

## Fish Species

### Day Fish (Peaceful, Common)
- **Sunfish** - Easy, colorful, friendly
- **Bass** - Medium, energetic
- **Trout** - Medium-hard, beautiful
- **Catfish** - Hard, bottom-dweller
- **Golden Carp** - Very rare, legendary beauty

### Night Creatures (Mysterious, Rare)
- **Pale Crawler** - Eerie, glowing, unsettling
- **Bleeding Carp** - Disturbing appearance, strange behavior
- **Whispering Eel** - Makes strange sounds, elusive
- **Fishman** - Legendary, terrifying, rare

## Mechanics

### Casting System
- **Power Meter**: 0-100%
- **Perfect Cast**: 50% power = bonus accuracy
- **Weather Effects**: Wind and rain affect distance
- **Skill Reward**: Better casts = better catches

### Reeling System
- **Tension Mechanic**: Fish pull on line
- **Line Strength**: Degrades during reeling
- **Difficulty Scaling**: Fish fight harder at night
- **Skill-Based**: Steady reeling = success

### Bait System
- **Worms**: Common fish, day fishing
- **Minnows**: Larger fish, medium difficulty
- **Cheese**: Catfish, specific species
- **Corn**: Rare fish, night fishing

## Atmosphere

### Day (6 AM - 6 PM)
- Bright, warm colors
- Peaceful music (if implemented)
- Friendly companion dialogue
- Common fish, easy catches
- Safe, welcoming environment

### Night (6 PM - 6 AM)
- Dark, cool colors
- Eerie ambient sounds
- Companion warnings
- Rare creatures, challenging catches
- Mysterious, slightly unsettling

### Transitions
- Sunset: Gradual color shift, companion gets nervous
- Sunrise: Relief, companion relaxes
- Weather: Affects both atmosphere and mechanics

## Companion System

### Personality
- Loyal and supportive
- Reacts to events (catches, anomalies, time of day)
- Provides warnings about dangerous situations
- Celebrates achievements

### Dialogue Examples
- **Day Fishing**: "Beautiful day for fishing!"
- **Rare Catch**: "Wow! That's incredible!"
- **Night Fishing**: "Be careful out here..."
- **Legendary Catch**: "I've never seen anything like that!"
- **Anomaly**: "Something feels... wrong..."

### Bonding Benefits
- Better fishing luck (higher catch rates)
- Emotional support (reduces stress)
- Special events (companion-specific moments)
- Unlocks endings (based on bond level)

## Cabin System

### Functions
- **Rest**: Advance time, restore energy
- **Save**: Automatic save on rest
- **Reflect**: Read journal, review catches
- **Decorate**: Customize cabin (future feature)
- **Safe Space**: Retreat from night fishing

### Progression
- Unlock cabin upgrades as you progress
- Decorations reflect your catches
- Companion has favorite spots in cabin

## Lore Framework

### The Lake's Secret
- Something changed the lake years ago
- Fish evolved into strange forms
- The night brings out their true nature
- Discovering the truth is optional but rewarding

### Environmental Storytelling
- Abandoned fishing equipment
- Strange marks on dock
- Unusual water patterns
- Companion's reactions to locations

### Multiple Endings
- **Peaceful Ending**: Enjoy fishing, ignore mysteries
- **Discovery Ending**: Uncover the lake's secrets
- **Companion Ending**: Achieve maximum bond
- **True Ending**: Catch all legendary fish and discover everything

## Design Principles

1. **Respect Player Time**: No forced horror, can always retreat
2. **Reward Exploration**: Discovering secrets feels rewarding
3. **Balance Cozy & Horror**: Neither overwhelms the other
4. **Progression Feels Good**: Constant sense of advancement
5. **Companion Matters**: Emotional connection to NPC
6. **Atmosphere Over Jumpscares**: Dread through environment
7. **Player Agency**: Choose your own pace and path
