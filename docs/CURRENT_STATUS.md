## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in browser
2. Press SPACE to fish (3 times to cast)
3. Watch the power meter fill up during casting
4. Wait for bite (bobber flashes red)
5. Press SPACE to hook and reel
6. Catch window shows your fish
7. Check journal (TAB) to see lore
